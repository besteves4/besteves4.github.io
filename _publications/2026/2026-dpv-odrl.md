abstract: >-
  This paper presents a machine-readable mapping between <a href="https://w3id.org/dpv" target="_blank">DPV</a> and the <a href="https://www.w3.org/TR/odrl-model/" target="_blank">ODRL</a> Information Model to bridge the gap between data protection concepts and enforceable usage control policies. By <a href="https://w3id.org/dpv/mappings/odrl" target="_blank">combining DPV's semantically rich vocabulary with ODRL's policy framework</a> and providing <a href="https://w3id.org/dpv/guides/dpv-odrl" target="_blank">implementation guidance</a>, it enables interoperable, legally grounded, and machine-processable usage policies that support trustworthy and compliant data sharing.
