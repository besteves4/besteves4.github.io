abstract: >-
  Limited transparency and ambiguous contractual clauses in SaaS Terms of Service make it difficult for customers to assess their rights, obligations, and potential risks. A semantic approach combining <a href="https://www.w3.org/TR/odrl-model/" target="_blank">ODRL</a>, <a href="https://w3id.org/dpv" target="_blank">DPV</a>, and rule-based reasoning enables machine-interpretable modelling of contractual terms and automated inference of risks arising from potentially unfair conditions.
