abstract: >-
  The <a href="https://data.europa.eu/eli/reg/2022/868/oj" target="_blank">EU Data Governance Act</a> defines distinct civic and commercial data-sharing pathways whose permissions, prohibitions, and obligations require machine-readable representation to support trustworthy, automated compliance. By modelling both pathways as <a href="https://www.w3.org/TR/odrl-model/" target="_blank">ODRL</a> policy chains enriched with <a href="https://w3id.org/dpv" target="_blank">DPV</a> and <a href="https://w3id.org/trustenvelope" target="_blank">Trust Envelope model</a>, the work demonstrates how end-to-end, machine-readable policy lifecycles can enable scalable data sharing and provide reusable patterns for future EU data governance frameworks.
