abstract: >-
  This work introduces <a href="https://w3id.org/force" target="_blank">FORCE</a>, a framework and web-based playground designed to support the development, testing, and interpretation of <a href="https://www.w3.org/TR/odrl-model/" target="_blank">ODRL</a> usage control policies in decentralized data-sharing systems. By building on a rigorously tested <a href="https://w3id.org/force/evaluator" target="_blank">ODRL Evaluator</a>, FORCE helps address interoperability challenges and improves understanding of policy evaluation outcomes.
