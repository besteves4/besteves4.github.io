abstract: >-
  This paper presents <a href="" target="_blank">LOAMA</a>, a low-code application for managing and enforcing <a href="https://www.w3.org/TR/odrl-model/" target="_blank">ODRL</a> policies in decentralised systems via an <a href="" target="_blank">UMA-based authorization server</a>. By abstracting policy complexity through an intuitive user interface, LOAMA enables non-experts to manage access preferences while supporting legal and economic requirements.
